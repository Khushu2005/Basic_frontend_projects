Impact:
  • Teaches self-discipline, resilience, and duty.
  • Provides a spiritual and philosophical perspective on life.
  • Helps in decision-making and self-realization.
Key Learnings:
  1. Do your duty without attachment to results.
  2. Face challenges with courage and faith.
  3. Control over the mind leads to true wisdom.
